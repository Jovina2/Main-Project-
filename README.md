TOPIC :- AI BASED FOOD ALLERGY PREDICTION 

Introduction

Food allergies are a serious and growing health concern that can cause severe and sometimes life-threatening reactions when allergenic foods are consumed accidentally. 
Many people find it difficult to identify hidden allergens in packaged or prepared foods due to complex ingredient lists. The AI-Based Food Allergy Prediction System is designed to help users identify potential
allergy risks in food before consumption using Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning algorithms such as Random Forest and Logistic Regression.  The system analyzes food ingredients along with the user’s 
allergy history to predict the risk level and provide instant alerts and safety recommendations. This project aims to improve food safety, awareness, and personal health management through intelligent prediction.
